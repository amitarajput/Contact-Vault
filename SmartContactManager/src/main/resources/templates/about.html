Contact Vault is a secure web application designed to help users manage their private contacts efficiently. The platform provides a personal space where users can store, organize, and access their favorite and essential contact information.

Key Features:

  • Add New Contacts: Users can easily add new contacts with relevant details, ensuring that their important connections are just a click away.
  • View and Manage Contacts: The application allows users to view their list of contacts, edit details, and organize them as needed.
  • User Profile: Each user has a personal profile page where they can manage their information and customize their experience.
  • Secure Access: The application is designed with user security in mind, ensuring that all contact information is kept private and protected.
  • User Authentication: Users must sign in to access their vault, keeping unauthorized users from viewing or altering their contact information.

Purpose: Contact Vault is tailored for individuals who value privacy and need a reliable solution for managing their personal and professional contacts in a secure environment.
